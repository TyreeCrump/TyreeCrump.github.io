GET TO KNOW TYREE CRUMP

Who is Tyree Crump?

Tyree Quanell Crump was born on September 12, 1997 in Bainbridge, Georgia. Tyree is the only child to Shantell Crump and Tyree Wilburn. At the early age of three, Tyree followed in his father’s footsteps and began playing basketball. As Tyree fell in love with game, he was committed to becoming great with the motivation of creating a better life for him and his mother.

Life as a Bearcat

In the fall of 2012, Crump began his high school career at Bainbridge High School. Tyree was starting point guard for the Bearcats varsity team from 2012-2016. During his time as a Bearcat, Tyree was outstanding player, scoring over 20 points on average each game, Tyree’s talent and abilities granted him 35 offers to D1 basketball programs across the nation. However in 2014, at the end of his sophomore year, Tyree committed to the University of Georgia. In 2016, Tyree was also nominated for the McDonald’s All-American Games.

Committed to the G

Tyree began his college career in 2016, under Coach Mark Fox. Tyree has been provided the opportunity to travel across the world with the Bulldogs to further enhance his skills for the collegiate level and beyond. Through out his college career, Tyree has remained humbled and has dedicated himself to becoming a better player and person.
